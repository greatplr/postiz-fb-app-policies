LinkedIn Integration

Privacy Policy for LinkedIn App

Effective Date: October 21, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Postiz ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our LinkedIn integration within the Postiz social media management application ("Service"). This policy supplements our general Privacy Policy and specifically addresses data handling for LinkedIn integration.

2. LinkedIn Data We Access

When you connect your LinkedIn account to Postiz, we request access to the following LinkedIn data through LinkedIn's official API:

Profile Information:

  • Your name, profile photo, and headline
  • Your LinkedIn profile URL
  • Basic profile information necessary for authentication

Content Permissions:

  • Permission to create and publish posts on your behalf
  • Permission to share articles, images, and videos
  • Access to post analytics and engagement metrics

Company Pages (if authorized):

  • List of LinkedIn company pages you have admin access to
  • Permission to post on behalf of company pages
  • Analytics data for company page posts

3. How We Use Your LinkedIn Data

We use the LinkedIn data we access solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Schedule and publish content to your LinkedIn profile or company pages as instructed by you
  • Retrieve analytics and engagement metrics for content you've published
  • Display your LinkedIn profile information within our Service
  • Provide customer support related to LinkedIn integration

We will never:

  • Post to LinkedIn without your explicit instruction or scheduled authorization
  • Access your LinkedIn messages or connection data
  • Share your LinkedIn credentials with third parties
  • Use your LinkedIn data for marketing purposes beyond the Service you've authorized

4. LinkedIn API Compliance

Our LinkedIn integration is built in strict compliance with LinkedIn's API Terms of Use. We:

  • Use only officially supported LinkedIn APIs
  • Respect LinkedIn's rate limits and usage guidelines
  • Store LinkedIn OAuth tokens securely with encryption
  • Automatically refresh tokens to maintain secure access
  • Immediately revoke access when you disconnect your LinkedIn account

5. Data Storage and Security

LinkedIn authentication tokens and related data are:

  • Encrypted both in transit (using TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Never logged in plain text
  • Automatically invalidated when you revoke access
  • Subject to regular security audits

6. Data Retention

We retain LinkedIn-related data as follows:

  • Authentication tokens: Retained while your LinkedIn account is connected; immediately deleted upon disconnection
  • Published content: Retained for historical reference and analytics until you delete it or close your account
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 12 months for reporting purposes
  • Profile information: Cached temporarily and refreshed periodically while connected

7. Disconnecting Your LinkedIn Account

You can disconnect your LinkedIn account from Postiz at any time by:

  • Visiting your account settings in Postiz and removing the LinkedIn connection
  • Revoking access directly from your LinkedIn account settings

When you disconnect, we will:

  • Immediately delete your LinkedIn authentication tokens
  • Stop all scheduled posts to LinkedIn
  • Cease accessing your LinkedIn data
  • Remove cached LinkedIn profile information

8. Third-Party Access

Your LinkedIn data is only shared with:

  • LinkedIn: To authenticate and publish content on your behalf
  • Our hosting providers: Who store encrypted data under strict confidentiality agreements
  • You: Through our analytics and reporting features

We do not sell, rent, or share your LinkedIn data with advertisers or other third parties.

9. Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:

  • Review what LinkedIn data we access at any time
  • Modify or revoke permissions granted to our app
  • Request a copy of your LinkedIn data stored by Postiz
  • Request deletion of your LinkedIn data from our systems
  • Disconnect your LinkedIn account without losing other Postiz features

10. LinkedIn's Privacy Policy

Your use of LinkedIn and the data LinkedIn collects about you is governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy, available at: https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy

We recommend reviewing LinkedIn's privacy policy to understand how LinkedIn handles your data independently of our Service.

11. Changes to LinkedIn Integration

If we make changes to how we handle LinkedIn data or request additional permissions, we will:

  • Notify you via email or in-app notification
  • Update this privacy policy with a new effective date
  • Request your consent for any new permissions required

12. Compliance and Certifications

Our LinkedIn integration and data handling practices comply with:

  • LinkedIn API Terms of Use
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Industry-standard security practices
