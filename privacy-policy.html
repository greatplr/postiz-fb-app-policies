Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 19, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Postiz social media management application ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and password.

Social Media Credentials: To provide our service, we collect and store authentication tokens and credentials for the social media platforms you connect (such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.).

Content Data: We store the content you create, schedule, and publish through our Service, including text, images, videos, and other media.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Service, including analytics data, scheduled posts, publishing history, and engagement metrics.

Technical Data: We automatically collect certain technical information including IP address, browser type, device information, and access times.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your access to connected social media platforms
  • Schedule and publish content on your behalf
  • Provide analytics and insights about your social media performance
  • Improve and optimize the Service
  • Communicate with you about the Service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party social media platforms including Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, and others. When you connect these accounts, you authorize us to access and use your data from these platforms in accordance with their respective terms of service and privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share your information with:

  • Social media platforms you've authorized us to access
  • Service providers who help us operate the Service (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure storage of authentication tokens
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or deleting your account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Export your data
  • Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyze how you use the Service. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
